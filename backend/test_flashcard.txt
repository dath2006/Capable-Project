Machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence. It focuses on developing algorithms that learn from data.

Supervised learning involves training models on labeled datasets.
Unsupervised learning finds hidden patterns in unlabeled data.
Reinforcement learning uses trial and error, rewarding the agent for beneficial actions.

Neural networks are mathematical models inspired by the human brain. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes called artificial neurons.
Deep learning uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers (deep networks) to model complex patterns.
